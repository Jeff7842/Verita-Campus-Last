Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our short courses

What is the duration of each course?

Our short courses typically range from 6 to 9 weeks, with most being 6 weeks long. Each course includes 3-5 hours of instruction per week, plus optional practical assignments.

Are the courses online or in-person?

We offer both online and in-person options for most courses. You can choose your preferred format during registration. Online courses include live sessions with instructors and access to recorded materials.

What certification will I receive?

Upon successful completion of a course, you'll receive a Verita Campus Professional Certificate that you can add to your LinkedIn profile and resume. Some courses also offer industry-recognized certifications.

Do I need prior experience to enroll?

Most of our courses are designed for beginners, with no prior experience required. For advanced courses, we clearly indicate any prerequisites in the course description.

What payment options are available?

We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. We also offer installment plans for most courses - please contact our admissions team for details.
