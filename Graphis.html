Comprehensive Curriculum

Our 6-week program covers all essential graphic design topics through hands-on projects

Week 1: Design Foundations
  • Principles of design (balance, contrast, hierarchy)
  • Color theory and psychology
  • Typography fundamentals
  • Introduction to Adobe Creative Cloud
  • Design thinking process
Week 2: Digital Tools Mastery
  • Adobe Photoshop for image editing
  • Adobe Illustrator for vector graphics
  • Adobe InDesign for layout design
  • Figma for UI/UX design
  • File formats and export settings
Week 3: Brand Identity Design
  • Logo design principles
  • Brand style guides
  • Business card and stationery design
  • Packaging design basics
  • Presenting design concepts
Week 4: Digital & Print Design
  • Social media graphics and templates
  • Poster and flyer design
  • Brochures and magazines
  • Print production basics
  • Designing for different mediums
Week 5: UI/UX & Web Design
  • User interface design principles
  • Wireframing and prototyping
  • Mobile app design
  • Website mockups
  • Design systems
Week 6: Portfolio Development
  • Curating your best work
  • Creating case studies
  • Online portfolio setup
  • Freelancing and job search strategies
  • Final project presentation
