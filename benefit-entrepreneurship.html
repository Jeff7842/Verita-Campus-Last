Entrepreneurship Support

Turn your ideas into reality with our comprehensive startup support system

From Idea to Impact

At Verita Campus, we believe that the ambitions of our learners are valuable. We provide the resources, guidance, and funding to help you turn your ideas into successful ventures.

Learners are able to achieve their ambitions through expert guidance and funding of their projects from incubation to completion. The whole process is also achieved through interactions with international companies and institutions that we collaborate with.

  • Idea validation and market research support
  • Business model development workshops
  • Access to seed funding and grants
  • Legal and financial advisory services
  • Prototyping and MVP development support

Why Choose Entrepreneurship?

Entrepreneurship is more than starting a business; it’s about creating value, solving real problems, and taking ownership of your future.

By choosing this path, you learn to turn ideas into action, adapt to challenges, and build solutions that matter to people.

You gain the freedom to shape your work around your values while developing resilience, creativity, and leadership along the way. Whether you aim to launch a tech startup, a creative studio, or a social enterprise, entrepreneurship equips you with the mindset and practical skills to drive impact and create a life and career on your own terms.

Incubation Program

Our startup incubation program provides selected ventures with workspace, mentorship, funding, and access to our network of investors and partners. The program runs for 6-12 months, depending on the needs of your business.

We've helped launch over 50 successful startups across various industries, with many going on to secure additional funding and achieve profitability.

  • Dedicated workspace and resources
  • Weekly mentorship sessions
  • Access to our investor network
  • Product development support
  • Marketing and sales strategy development

Define Your Own Success

Success isn't one-size-fits-all. We guide you to define what success means for you, then give you the tools and support to reach it through consistent action and measurable milestones.

Through our structured success frameworks, you will build habits and systems that align with your goals, allowing you to grow sustainably and confidently in your chosen path.

  • Personalized goal-setting frameworks
  • Weekly accountability check-ins
  • Real-world challenges to test progress
  • Access to mentors and a supportive community
