Comprehensive Curriculum

Our 6-week intensive program covers all essential finance and accounting topics

Week 1: Financial Literacy Fundamentals
  • Understanding personal finance in a global context
  • Budgeting techniques for individuals and households
  • Banking products and services
  • Introduction to credit and debt management
  • Basic investment concepts for beginners
Week 2: Accounting Principles
  • Double-entry bookkeeping system
  • Chart of accounts and ledger maintenance
  • Recording financial transactions
  • Understanding debits and credits
  • Accounting standards and regulations
Week 3: Financial Statements & Analysis
  • Preparing balance sheets
  • Creating income statements
  • Cash flow statement fundamentals
  • Financial ratio analysis
  • Interpreting financial reports
Week 4: Taxation & Compliance
  • Tax system overview
  • Personal income tax calculations
  • Corporate tax requirements
  • VAT and withholding tax
  • Tax compliance and filing procedures
Week 5: Business Finance
  • Financial planning for businesses
  • Working capital management
  • Costing and pricing strategies
  • Capital budgeting techniques
  • Financing options for businesses
Week 6: Digital Finance & Final Project
  • Fintech innovations
  • Mobile money and digital banking
  • Accounting software (QuickBooks, Sage)
  • Financial data visualization
  • Capstone project: Financial analysis case study
