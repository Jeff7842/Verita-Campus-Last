Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Verita Campus

Do I need high school grades or a university degree to enroll for the courses?

NO, you don't need high school papers or a degree to study the courses in Verita Campus. All you need is to be willing to grow as you learn the skills of the profession you choose.

Is Verita Campus good for me as an entrepreneur?

YES, this is the perfect fit for you. Our learning sessions not only focus on career, but also emphasizes on entrepreneurship. We help learners grow by exposing them to Professionals and skills that equip them to be better at entrepreneurship.

Can I get a job with the certificates from Verita Campus?

SURE, you can get a job using the certificates acquired, alongside meeting the recruiters' requirements. Our qualifications are internationally accredited, having high value standard worldwide.

What exactly is Verita Avenue?

Verita Avenue is an extensive coursework that takes 6 months of completion once a learner has enrolled. It equips learners with deep and intensive mastery of skills in the course that they enroll in. These are not only classes, but opportunities for learners to gain global connections with seasoned professionals.

Do I have to pay to become a Verita Alumni?

NO, we do not charge money to add learners in the Verita Alumni Community. Once you have graduated from the coursework, you are eligible to become a Verita alumnus.

Is there an age limit for studying in Verita?

NO. There is no age restriction for enrolling for our courses. Anyone with an interest to learn and grow is accepted to study the courses.
