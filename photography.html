Comprehensive Curriculum

Our 7-week program covers all essential photography topics through hands-on shooting

Week 1: Camera Fundamentals
  • Understanding exposure triangle (aperture, shutter speed, ISO)
  • Camera modes and settings
  • Lens types and their uses
  • Basic composition techniques
  • File formats (RAW vs JPEG)
Week 2: Mastering Light
  • Natural light photography
  • Golden hour and blue hour
  • Artificial lighting setups
  • Light modifiers (diffusers, reflectors)
  • Flash photography basics
Week 3: Composition & Creativity
  • Rule of thirds and other composition rules
  • Leading lines and framing
  • Color theory in photography
  • Creating depth and dimension
  • Developing your photographic eye
Week 4: Portrait & People Photography
  • Posing techniques
  • Environmental portraits
  • Studio lighting setups
  • Working with models
  • Capturing authentic expressions
Week 5: Landscape & Travel Photography
  • Scouting locations
  • Working with natural light
  • Filters and long exposures
  • Creating panoramic images
  • Travel photography storytelling
Week 6: Specialized Photography
  • Product and food photography
  • Event and wedding photography
  • Street photography techniques
  • Wildlife and nature photography
  • Architectural photography
Week 7: Editing & Business
  • Adobe Lightroom workflow
  • Photoshop for photographers
  • Creating a professional portfolio
  • Pricing your photography services
  • Marketing your photography business
